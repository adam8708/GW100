Art As Rememberance

At #GreatWar100 we will be unveiling a unique art installation as a memorial to those who participated in the Great War.


But we can't create it alone. You can ensure that your ancestor or research subject is part of the piece by providing us with a photograph of them.


There is no cost for their inclusion, but you can help with the creation of this one-off act of commemoration by making a donation below.


There will be a list of contributors both materially and monetary produced to accompany the piece. There will also be a dedication produced including the names of all those featured.


Things you should know Before you donate an image

  • The images will be used once, in hard format.
  • They will not be reproduced, digitally or otherwise beyond the chance that they may appear in a wider shots of the installation.
  • Please make sure that your images are as high in quality as possible. In cases of extremely low quality/obvious pixilation we may not be able to use your image.
  • No, your submission does not need to have served in the army, the Royal Navy or the flying services. We welcome images of nurses, munitions workers, anyone who played an active part in the conflict.
  • Yes, you can donate more than one image. If this makes it prohibitive to use email, please contact us to arrange another method of submission.
  • When donating an image you must cut and past the following line into your email:


    • "I certify that I have the right to reproduce this image and that I give permission for it to be used in the manner described by #GreatWar100"

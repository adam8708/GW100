Keep This Event Free Donate

We do not think anyone should have to pay to commemorate the centenary. There is no cost to attend #GreatWar100, but lectures will be ticketed. Donating to help stage the event can guarantee your place before we begin processing applications.

£25 - Earns you the option to take early bird guaranteed admission for one to all lectures on one date.

£50 - Double up and earn the option to take early bird guaranteed admission for two to all lectures on one date.

£100 earns you the option to take early bird guaranteed admission for the whole family: four tickets to all of our lectures on one date

£250 is the big one. You will get the option to take early bird guaranteed admission to all lectures on one date and a pitch in our Marketplace for sales, or for your WW1 organisation to secure a pop-up booth to engage with the public on both days.

All profits will be donated to Veterans in Action, registered charity #1128026

Engage Customers/Grow Your Organisation Sales Opportunities

Retail: Whether its antiquarian books, militaria, battlefield tours, memorabilia, art or miscellany; £200 secures a merchandise stall in our market place for both sessions.

Growth: £100 guarantees you a space for a pop-up information booth - recruit members for your WW1 group, bring your WW1 project to a wider audience or collect for a WW1 related cause.

Partner Up: Interested in joining our lists of corporate sponsors? Drop us a line so we can talk about how you could benefit from joining our team.

Friday 9th & Saturday 10th November 2018 Remembering the Great War

#GreatWar100 will bring together members of the public who have developed an interest in the First World War since 2014, unite them with experts in the field and not only provide an informal education experience, but ensure that moving forward past November 1918, we continue to remember.

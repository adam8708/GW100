Apply For Tickets

Any purchased tickets will be distributed electronically to successful applicants in mid-October.


We do not think anyone should have to pay to commemorate the centenary. There is no cost to attend #GreatWar100, but talks and children's events will be ticketed. Donating to help stage the event can guarantee your place before we begin processing applications.


£25 - Earns you the option to take early bird guaranteed admission for one to all talks on one date.


£50 - Double up and earn the option to take early bird guaranteed admission for two to all talks on one date.


£100 - Earns you the option to take early bird, guaranteed admission for the whole family: four tickets to all of our talks on one date and guaranteed admission to childrens' activities.


£250 - Not only will you get the option to take early bird guaranteed admission to all talks on one date. You will also be entitled to a pitch in our Marketplace for sales, or for your WW1 organisation to secure a pop-up booth to engage with the public on both days. You will also be entitled to two places on the guest list at a special commemorative after-event.


All profits will be donated to Veterans in Action, registered charity #1128026


Rembemer, it is absolutely free to come along and experience #GreatWar100 without tickets. It is only necessary to apply if you wish to attend any talks or workshops for children.
